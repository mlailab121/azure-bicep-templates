Create Resource Group
az group create --name rg-arm-demo --location eastus

Validate Bicep Template
az deployment group validate --resource-group rg-arm-demo --template-file azuredeploy.json --parameters @azuredeploy.parameters.json

What-If Deployment - Preview changes before applying:
az deployment group what-if --resource-group rg-arm-demo --template-file azuredeploy.json --parameters @azuredeploy.parameters.json

Deploy ARM Template
az deployment group create --name storage-deploy-01 --resource-group rg-arm-demo --template-file azuredeploy.json --parameters @azuredeploy.parameters.json

az deployment group validate --resource-group TestLab-Ruwaz --template-file create-vm.json
